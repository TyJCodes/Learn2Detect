Social engineering

Here you will learn about the main types of social engineering.

Phishing

What is it?

Phishing is when an attacker sends fake emails or text that look like they are from "trusted sources" e.g. amaz0n.com instead of amazon.com.

How to spot it?

To spot a phishing email, check if it is making you urgently do something, if it is coming from an actual trusted source, and hover over links to see if they are real.

Fun fact

Phishing emails are slightly obvious as most of them tend to require the user to do stuff later. They make it semi obvious to weed out people who will catch on later.

Tail gating

What is it?

Following someone in person in order to gain sneak into a place they are unauthorized to.

How to spot it?

Someone without the correct identification trying to ask you to hold the door because their hands are full.

Fun fact

People stole a bunch of money doing this, by pretending they were construction workers. Ultimately they were caught.

Baiting

What is it?

A "free" lure, like a USB stick labeled secret in a car park.

How to spot it?

Unknown devices or free downloads that seem too good to be true.

Fun fact

Nearly 50% of people plug in random USBs they find.

Pre texting

What is it?

An attacker uses a fake story to pretend to be IT or authority.

How to spot it?

Asking for sensitive information that they should already have.

Fun fact

Many attackers will spend weeks researching your life first in order to pull off a successful attack.
